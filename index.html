We've Got You Covered!

Without proper health insurance, injury or illness can feel like walking a tightrope without a safety net. The Student Health Insurance staff is here to ensure you have all the information needed to make the right decision about your health care coverage needs.

Our staff is dedicated to ensuring you know exactly what your Student Health Insurance is, how it works, and the many ways in which it benefits you. We serve as your on-campus advocates for your healthcare needs, outside of what McKinley Health Center is able to provide.

United Healthcare StudentResources and HealthiestYou have partnered together to provide access to doctors and mental healthcare from anywhere you are, even while traveling internationally. All services are FREE for students covered under the UHCSR insurance plan; services are available for all other students for a fee.
